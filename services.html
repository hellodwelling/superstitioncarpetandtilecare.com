Our Services

What we are best at - expert residential & commercial cleaning

Carpet Cleaning

Our Carpet Cleaning service is a great way to get your carpets looking and smelling fresh again. We use powerful equipment and eco-friendly solutions to clean every nook and cranny of your carpets, so they look like new again.

Tile and Grout Cleaning

Our Tile and Grout Cleaning service is the perfect way to clean and refresh your tile floors. Our experienced professionals will use a special cleaning solution to remove dirt, dust, and grime from your tiles and grout lines. We'll also seal your grout for a longer-lasting clean.

Carpet Repairs

Our Carpet Repair service is perfect for fixing any small to medium-sized damages that your carpets may have. We'll take a look at the damage and determine the best way to fix it, so your carpets look good as new again! Call us for additional details.

Upholstery Cleaning

Our Upholstery Cleaning service is the perfect way to keep your furniture looking like new. We use special equipment and detergents to remove dirt, dust, and stains from your upholstered furniture.

Pet Urine and Odor Removal

If you're experiencing pet urine or odor in your home, we can help! Our team will use chemicals such as USR (Urine Stain Removal) and EOC (Enzyme Odor Counteractant) to remove all traces of the mess - and prevent it from happening again. For bigger pet stains please call us for additional details.

24 Hour Flood Extraction

We provide 24 Hour Flood Extraction service to help homeowners quickly and efficiently remove water from their property. We are available 24/7 to help you get your home back to normal as soon as possible.
